I'm now a 4th year Ph.D. student in Computer Science, work with Professor Frank Dellaert and Professor Byron Boots at Georgia Institute of Technology. Prior to joining Georgia Tech, I got my Bachelor degree in Engineering Mechanics and Aerospace Engineering from Tsinghua University, Beijing, China.

My Current research interest includes various topics in Robotics and Computer Vision, which cover but are not limited to Simultaneous Localization and Mapping (SLAM), Structure from Motion, Sensor Fusion, and Motion Planning.
